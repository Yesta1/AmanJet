Read More
  • AmanJet+ is our premium tier that unlocks advanced safety features including real-time incident alerts, journey tracking, personalised safety scores, and priority access to our 24/7 support team. It's designed for individuals and organisations that need enhanced protection.
  • The Safety Map is an interactive, real-time layer that shows verified safe zones, reported incidents, live street support locations, and trusted venues in your area. It updates continuously so you always have the latest safety picture.
  • You can report suspicious activity, hazards, incidents, unsafe environments, and any safety concerns in your area. Reports are reviewed and, where appropriate, shared with relevant authorities and the wider AmanJet community.
  • Live Street Support shows the real-time locations of verified support personnel, safety officers, and partner organisations who can provide immediate assistance. Tapping a pin connects you directly with the nearest available support.
